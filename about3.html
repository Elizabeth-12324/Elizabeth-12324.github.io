About

Hello!

I'm Elizabeth! As a digital media artist with a background in computer science, I'm always exploring new intersections between technology and design! Some of these include game design, AR development, social media management, illustration, and streaming production! I've also worked extensivly within the field of audio research using machine learning!

Having grown up in Queens, New York, I strongly believe in giving back to the community that raised me and expanding my reach to guide rising students within the STEAM field. Over the years, I've developed various free educational programs and workshops for students across the world. My hard work and passion for community outreach has earned me an Educational Outreach Award on behalf of NASA for my leading role in mentoring future generations of engineers!

My love for animation and storytelling are never far from me since in my free time, I LOVE playing video games and watching movies! I can't wait to continue working on projects that can one day inspire audiences the same way I have been since childhood!
